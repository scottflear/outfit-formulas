# Outfit Formulas — Funnel Adaptation Map

## How Each Winning Architecture Translates to Outfit Formulas

### Funnel A: "Your Style Score" — Closet Paralytic Focus
**Original:** SmartyMe (learning app, countdown + trial hybrid)
**OF Quiz Theme:** "What's Your Hidden Outfit Score?" — How many unseen outfits are hiding in your closet?
**Target Archetype:** The Closet Paralytic
**Quiz Length:** 25-30 screens (medium)
**Key Adaptation:**
- Replace learning metrics with wardrobe metrics (% of closet worn, morning time, outfit repeats)
- Score = "Hidden Outfit Score" (how many outfits she's NOT seeing)
- Countdown + trial hybrid paywall: 1-week $0.99 trial OR direct monthly/annual
- Live ticker: "782 women discovered hidden outfits this hour"
- Guarantee: 30-day money-back
**Why it works for OF:** The countdown creates urgency around her specific score. She just learned she has 50+ outfits she can't see — the timer says "act now or lose this insight."

### Funnel B: "Your Wardrobe Assessment" — Weight Waiter Focus
**Original:** Unimeal (weight loss, longest quiz, sunk-cost redirect)
**OF Quiz Theme:** "Complete Style Assessment" — Deep-dive into her wardrobe, body, life
**Target Archetype:** The Weight Waiter
**Quiz Length:** 34-40 screens (longest — maximum sunk cost)
**Key Adaptation:**
- Replace weight/diet questions with wardrobe/body-change/shopping-habit questions
- Email gate after results: "Enter your email to unlock your personalized style plan and see your closet potential"
- Paywall on SEPARATE page (not in quiz flow) — feels like "accessing your results" not "buying"
- Weight-waiting reframe: "You don't need to wait for a different body to dress well"
- Savings projection: "Average women waste $2,000/year on unworn clothes. Your formula saves you $X"
**Why it works for OF:** The Weight Waiter has the deepest shame and the longest decision cycle. The 34+ screen investment makes her feel like she's already started the process — abandoning now means wasting all that self-reflection.
